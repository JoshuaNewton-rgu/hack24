Duthie Park Regulations and Policies:

  1. General Rules:
    • Park hours: The park is open from 0:00 to 24:00. However Winter Gardens and The Park Cafe has different openings. No entry is allowed outside of those hours.
    • Toilet: there is a chargeable Automated Public Convenience in the park which is available at all times.
    • All visitors must comply with posted signs, rules, and regulations.
  2. Environmental Conservation:
    • Leave No Trace: Visitors are required to clean up after themselves and leave no litter. Dispose of waste in designated bins.
    • Flora and Fauna: Do not disturb or harm plant and animal life. Feeding wildlife is strictly prohibited.
  3. Recreational Activities:
    • Picnicking: Use designated picnic areas. Open fires and barbecues are allowed only in designated areas.
    • Sports and Games: Play sports and games in designated areas only, respecting the safety and comfort of others.
  4. Safety and Security:
    • Emergency Procedures: Familiarize yourself with emergency procedures and exits. In case of an emergency, follow park staff instructions.
    • First Aid: Report any injuries or emergencies to park staff immediately. First aid stations are located at Winter Gardens' entrance.
  5. Pets:
    • Owners are responsible for cleaning up after their pets. Pet waste must be properly disposed of in designated bins.
    • Ensure that pets do not disturb or endanger other visitors, wildlife, or park property.
    • If a pet displays aggressive behavior, including excessive barking, lunging, or biting, it must be immediately leashed and removed from the park
    • Pets must be in good health and have up-to-date vaccinations. Display visible identification tags with contact information.
  6. Noise Control:
    • Keep noise levels at a minimum to ensure a peaceful environment for all visitors.
    • Quiet hours may be enforced during specific times. Please be considerate of others.
  7. Special Events and Permits:
    • Organized events and gatherings require prior approval and a valid permit from park authorities.
    • Photography and Filming: Commercial photography and filming require a permit.
  8. Vehicle Usage:
    • Follow designated paths and roadways for vehicles. Speed limits must be adhered to at all times.
    • Parking: Use designated parking areas. Illegally parked vehicles may be towed at the owner's expense.
  9. Accessibility:
    • The park strives to be accessible to all visitors. Please notify park staff of any special needs or concerns.
  10. Enforcement and Penalties:
    • Violation of park regulations may result in fines, expulsion from the park, or legal action.
    • Park authorities reserve the right to refuse entry to any individual or group not complying with park rules.
